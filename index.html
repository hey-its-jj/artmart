ArtMart is...
The Internet's best art supply marketplace!

Shopping for art supplies can be a pain. Sometimes you can't find the right tool for the job, other times they're way too expensive. Let us help you do what you love! ArtMart is an easy-to-use art supply resale app where you can buy and sell used supplies.

The Basics

The essential functions of the app.

Sort

Organize our catalogue by artistic discipline and tool to make browsing easier. Depending on what you're looking for, you can further filter based on a variety of parameters.

Buy

Buy from and communicate with trusted sellers. Keep track of your orders from the Home screen.

Sell

List your supplies for sale and let us take care of the shipping. We won't take too much from your profits, so don't worry about breaking even!

Why ArtMart?

Here's what makes us special.

A VARIED SELECTION

Since so much of our marketplace is comprised of shoppers like you all around the world, there'll always be someone who's selling what you need. Products that are long discontinued or hard to come by find a home on ArtMart.

WE'RE ON YOUR SIDE

We'll only take 5% of any sale you close to make using ArtMart worth your while. We also prioritize business with trustworthy vendors so you can feel confident in your purchase.

WE SHIP ANYWHERE

It's often the case in art supply resale that you have to meet your vendors in person or go to a brick-and-mortar location to do business. Not so with ArtMart: Shipping is quick, affordable and easy so you can get your hands on any item anywhere.
